Summary

This show is a retelling of bible events as they happen, where our main characters cause most of the problems in a Forrest Gump kind of way. The first season of this show follows the story of Armageddon, where our main characters try to influence the life of the antichrist, to make him a neutral entity rather than one of evil, while also trying to keep the trust of heaven and hell, respectively. Along the way, we get snippets of past events from the beginning of humanity, and sometimes even before that.
This show is based on a book written by Terry Prattchet and Neil Gaiman, and then continued for a second (and hopefully third) season by Neil Gaiman.

Why I Like This Show

This show is a comfort show for me, and I’ve come to know the characters beyond the page and the show. I love the fandom surrounding this show, and I love the writing of both this and the book. I recommend this show to anyone who’s looking for something to watch at any chance I get.
